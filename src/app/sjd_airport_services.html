SJD AIRPORT SERVICES

Parking
SJD Airport has 3 parking lots with capacity for 374 vehicles. Price is $15 pesos per hour. day parking is $150 pesos.

Medical service
There is medical service on dutty 24/7, with specialized staff. Rom for emergency and ambulance type One for emergency trasnfers.

Lost & Found
Passengers leaving items on airplanes should contact the airline directly. If items were left in the terminal area or passenger security screening areas, you should contact  the airport autority (phone numbers & contact information in this page).

Banking & ATMs
There are 2 money exchange offices located in Terminal T-1 and Terminal T-2 as well as an ATM in each terminal T1 & T2
